MEET THE ARTIST

Liron Tako

I am a multidisciplinary creator driven by the dialogue between the hand and the machine. My work is an exploration of textures—the softness of wool, the rigidity of 3D-printed plastic, and the organic transformation of melting wax.

Every piece I create is a personal story, tailored to those who seek art that isn't just seen, but felt. From unique home decor to personalized gift narratives, my mission is to bring a sense of sanctuary into every home.
